That diaspora capital — when structured properly — can transform the food economy back home and create meaningful, lasting impact.

The Process

How a Funding Cycle Works

Every cycle is time-bound, slot-limited, and structured for clarity — so participants always know exactly where their capital is.

Create Account
Register and complete KYC verification to unlock participation access.
2

3

Trust & Compliance

Built to Operate Responsibly

Genesis360 operates within a clear regulatory and ethical framework — protecting participants and ensuring every cycle is accountable.

🏛️
CAC Registered
Genesis360 is a formally registered Nigerian company with full CAC documentation. All operations are conducted under a legally recognized entity.
🔐
KYC & Identity Verification
Every participant completes identity verification before accessing any funding cycle. NIN, BVN, and document checks ensure a clean, verified participant base.
📋
No Guaranteed Returns
Genesis360 is a participation platform, not an investment product. All cycle participation is voluntary and participants understand the structured nature of the program.
🔒
Secure Infrastructure
Built on enterprise-grade infrastructure. All financial transactions processed via Paystack. Participant data protected with industry-standard encryption.
